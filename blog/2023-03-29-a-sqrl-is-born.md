After 18 months of labor, we are excited and relieved to announce the [0.1.0 release of DataSQRL](/). Shout it from the rooftops: A SQRL is Born! Which is also the official name of this release.

DataSQRL 0.1.0 is available on Dockerhub. This is our first official release of DataSQRL. It's ready to be used, and we look forward to your feedback.

DataSQRL is *not yet* ready for production. We want to get some more feedback from the community and test additional use cases, before we can recommend it for production use.

<!--truncate-->

So, what's in this release? Well, everything. It contains the compiler for SQRL, the optimizer, and pipeline generator.
